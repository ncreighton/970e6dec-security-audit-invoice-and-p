You've completed the vulnerability scan, penetration test, and compliance check. Now you're stuck chasing invoices and waiting for payments. That ends today.

This plugin bridges the gap between security audit delivery and payment collection. Instead of manually creating invoices after every audit, it automates the entire billing process—so you can focus on finding vulnerabilities, not chasing payments. It's the only WordPress plugin designed specifically for security auditors who want to streamline invoicing and get paid faster.

## What's Included

- One-click invoice generation upon audit completion (supports vulnerability scan, penetration test, compliance check results)
- Integrated payment gateway (Stripe, PayPal, etc.) for instant online payments
- Client portal where clients can view audit reports and payment history
- Custom invoice templates with your logo, audit details, and payment terms
- Automated email reminders for overdue invoices and payment confirmations

## Who Is This For

- Freelance security auditors who need to bill clients after each penetration test
- Small security firms managing multiple audit projects and invoices
- MSPs offering security audit add-ons and want automated billing
- Compliance consultants who require professional invoicing for compliance checks

## How It Works

Install the plugin on your WordPress site, connect your preferred payment gateway, and configure your audit templates. After each security audit, the plugin automatically generates a professional invoice attached to the audit report. Clients receive a payment link and can pay online—you get notified instantly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does this plugin work with existing WordPress audit tools?**
Yes, it integrates with popular WordPress audit and security plugins. You can also manually trigger invoice generation for audits done outside WordPress.

**What payment methods are supported?**
Stripe and PayPal are built-in. Additional gateways can be added via hooks.

**Can I customize invoice templates?**
Absolutely. You can edit the invoice layout, add your logo, and change payment terms directly from the settings.

**Is client data secure?**
Yes, all payment and audit data is encrypted and stored securely following WordPress best practices.

**How do I handle refunds or partial payments?**
The plugin includes a refund management interface. You can issue full or partial refunds from the admin dashboard.

3. Configure via Settings > Security & Audit Invoice and Payment WordPress Plugin
